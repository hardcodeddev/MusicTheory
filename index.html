Dubstep playbook

Cheat Codes

Rhythm

  • Think in half-time: snares on beat 3, leaving space for bass.
  • Use triplets and swung sixteenths to create movement.
  • Layer percussion with subtle low-pass filters to keep it dark.

Harmony

  • Minor keys and modal interchange (♭VII, iv, ♭VI) create grit.
  • Use suspended chords before drops for tension.
  • Try chromatic mediants for surprising lifts.

Sound design

  • Automate filter cutoff with LFOs synced to dotted 8ths.
  • Layer FM bass with sub sine to keep the low-end solid.
  • Use pitch envelopes for growls and vocal-like articulations.
